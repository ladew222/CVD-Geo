This data viewer uses data from Tom Quisel's COVID-19 database and provides a variety of ways to look at the day data. The goal is to allow better understanding of county level data. It should not replace advice and expertise of the CDC and other state and governmental agencies. It currently being developed and data validation has not yet occurred. There are some counties that are missing in the display which will hopefully be resolved soon. The Pearson test of correlation also is not yet built to handle null values.
